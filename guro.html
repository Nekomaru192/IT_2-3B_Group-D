WHAT IS GURO CARD?

  • The Guro edition of kaibigan Card is a discount card for our beloved public & private teacher.
  • Every cardholder is entitled of 5% discount on school supplies.
  • Validity: Up to 2 years upon availing the card.

Requirements for availing the card:

  1. Faculty and/or PRC I.D.
  2. Fill up registration form from Pandayan Bookshop
  3. P25.00 registration fee if with PRC I.D; P50.00 if without PRC ID.
This card is for all presently teaching public & private teachers.
