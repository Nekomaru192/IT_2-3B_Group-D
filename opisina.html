WHAT IS OPISINA CARD?

  • The Opisina Edition of the Kaibigan Card is a discount card for offices like accounting firms, government offices, restaurants, and many other business establishment. The card is valid up to 2 years upon availing.
  • Every cardholder is entitled of 5% discount on school & offices supplies for every P500 minimimum worth of purchase.

Requirements for availing the card:

  1. Original and Photocopy of business permit
  2. Fill up registration form from Pandayan Bookshop
  3. P50.00 registration fee
